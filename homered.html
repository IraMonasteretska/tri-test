Sports injuries can be devastating. Not only are you injured and have to heal, but it forces you out of the game. Injury rehabilitation can follow corrective surgery or non-invasive procedures. Different bodies respond to injuries in a variety of ways. In the same sense, you need a personalized approach to exercise regimens and rehabilitation because your body heals in its own way.

A sports injury clinic has to have the knowledge, equipment and methods available to tailor a rehabilitation plan to your specific needs and challenges. Your physical therapy practice has everything you need to get back on the road to recovery and strong health.

TRI Physical Therapy (PT) offers a hands-on, one-on-one approach to physical rehabilitation, improve movement and manage pain. Our top rated physical therapists provide a fundamentally new form of innovative and personalized rehabilitation care. At our center we combine traditional techniques for back, shoulder, hip, knee, ankle pain with alternative treatments, and cutting-edge chiropractic services. Contact us for effective treatment and proven preventative steps.

Our specialists have the most advanced training, are part of the Olympic medical team, USA triathlon, USA swimming team, New York road runners, and are the most experienced and best physical therapists in Brooklyn (PT).

Our center is a one-stop practice for all your rehabilitation needs. Our specialists are widely regarded, the techniques are cutting-edge and the range of services is comprehensive. You never have to go to another practice when you’re looking for a therapy.

MORE TREATMENTS AVAILABLE

If you’re competitive, you can receive personalize training advice and guidance. If you’re trying to overcome your pain or range of motion limitations, physiotherapy procedures can get you there.

In addition to the modalities already listed, we offer:

SPORTS PHYSICAL THERAPY

To get you back in the game at your optimal performance level

MARATHON RECOVERY TREATMENT

To restore your body - particularly your hips, legs and feet - after running a grueling marathon

MYOFASCIAL PAIN TREATMENT

Which loosens the fascia surrounding your muscles and organs to relieve pain

ISOKINETIC EXERCISES

To build strength, develop endurance and improve your range of motion in a specific area of your body

BALANCE PHYSICAL THERAPY

To improve your balance for sports or to avoid future falls

SPINAL DECOMPRESSION TREATMENT EXERCISES

Which stretches your spine to reduce pain caused by impinged spinal nerves

Take Control of Your Health

SPINAL AND JOINT MANIPULATION

Targeted therapy to ease back or joint pain while teaching you proper movement and posture

FRACTURE REHABILITATION

Therapy to help you overcome a broken bone while strengthening the muscles around it

SHOCKWAVE THERAPY

Which uses ultrasound to help your body heal more quickly

TRIGENICS THERAPY

A new way to treat pain and performance issues

LASER THERAPY

To encourage your body to use its natural ability to heal itself

VESTIBULAR THERAPY AND TRAINING

To naturally cure any balance issues you face, particularly from your inner ear
